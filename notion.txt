Notion Introduction:
Notion is a productivity tool used for creating notes, managing tasks and organizing work in one place. 
It is used for documentation and provides free hosting services for publishing pages.

Benefits of Notion:
* Helps keep all notes and tasks organized in one place
* Useful for documentation and personal or team projects
* Supports collaboration – multiple people can work together
* Provides templates to save time and quickly create pages
* Allows customization – add icons, cover images, headings, tables, etc.
